About the project

Turning "No" into Know

We discovered this problem during the Mandi field immersion exercise. Watching real vendors and customers, one gap stood out: businesses obsess over the sales they win, but almost never study the ones they lose.

The problem

Businesses track successful sales but rarely analyze customer rejections — so the single richest source of improvement walks out the door unrecorded.

The solution

WHY NOT? captures customer objections at the moment they happen and transforms them into actionable business intelligence: clear patterns, ranked reasons, and concrete recommendations a small team can act on the same day.
